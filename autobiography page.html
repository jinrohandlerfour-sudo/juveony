Autobiography

I was born with a strong curiosity and passion for learning. Growing up, I developed an interest in reading, writing, and exploring different cultures. These interests inspired me to pursue a Bachelor of Arts in English Studies, where I aim to refine my skills in communication, literature, and critical thinking.

Some key experiences that shaped me include working on group projects, learning foreign languages, and discovering the power of technology in education. My goal is to become a professional in the field of language, communication, or education while continuing to grow as a lifelong learner.
